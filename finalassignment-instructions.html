14. For row 21, repeat step 4.
15. For row 22, repeat step 3.

17. For row 24, create 5 HDC, then chain 13 stitches, then do 3 HDC, then chain 13 stitches, then do 3 HDC, then chain 13 stitches, and finish off with 5 HDC.
18. Then create 8 rows of HDC, the total number of rows by the end should be 32.
